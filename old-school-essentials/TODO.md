## Design Principles to Maintain

- **Gold-for-mechanical-benefit** (like philanthropy's 80% XP conversion)
- **Dice-based outcomes** (like carousing's venue costs)
- **Concrete consequences, not vague obligations** (no "schemes" players can ignore)
- **Social/factional gameplay** emphasis
- **Downtime creates adventure hooks** and player investment
- **Consistent with OSE B/X philosophy** (streamlined, purposeful)

- Keep the Keep as the central hub for all downtime activities
